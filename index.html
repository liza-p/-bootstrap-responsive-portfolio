About me

I am a student of UC Berkeley Full Stack development bootcamp. Before a coding bootcamp I worked as a Quality Assurance Engineer for startups and middle size companies in San Francisco. When designing a front-end project I strive for a good user experience considering the diversity of users who will use it. When approaching the backend, I focus on creating quality products that translate to responsive front-end environments. I enjoy collaborating with teams to design products that enhance the lives of others. In my free time, I enjoy traveling, hiking and exploring new restaurants with my friends.
