About me

Kittou is very funny and playful, she likes to chase her toys, eat a lot of seafood and simply chill out with her owners. She needs to play with someone a little bit every day and of course, she is a sleeping champion too!

Recently Kittou turned 1 year and 8 month. She was born on the streets of San Francisco and lived there until the age of 5-6 month, acording to the vet. One day she wondered into a random house chasing the big bird and decided to hang for a while. This is how she found her forever home!
